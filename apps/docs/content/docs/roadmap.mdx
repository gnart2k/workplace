## Why a Live Roadmap?

By using Kaneo to manage our own roadmap, we:

- **Practice what we preach** - We use our own tool for project management
- **Provide transparency** - Real-time visibility into our development process
- **Enable collaboration** - Community members can see progress and provide feedback
- **Demonstrate features** - Showcase Kaneo's capabilities in action
